# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always think twice before making any online payment  
- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone, no matter what  
- Remember: you don’t need to scan a QR code to receive money  
- Check the name carefully before sending money to anyone  
- Avoid clicking on unknown links or messages  
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store  
- Keep your phone and apps updated for better security  
- Don’t use public Wi-Fi for banking or payments  

## UPI Safety Tips
- Never approve payment requests from unknown people  
- If someone asks for money urgently, verify it first  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call Helpline: 1930
